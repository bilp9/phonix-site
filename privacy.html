Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 2025

At Pierre Consulting Group LLC, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Phonix (“Software”) handles your data.

1. Local-Only Processing

All audio files and transcripts you process with Phonix remain on your device. We do not upload, transmit, or store your data on external servers.

2. Personal Information

The Software does not collect or transmit personally identifiable information. Your use of the app is entirely local unless you explicitly choose to connect to third-party services in the future.

3. Licensing and Activation

If license activation is required, the Software may validate your license key by communicating with our secure servers. No audio or personal content is transmitted.

4. Website Analytics

Our website (www.phonixplayer.com) may use analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics) to collect anonymous usage statistics, such as page visits and traffic sources. This information does not include audio or transcription data.

5. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect any data exchanged for licensing or support purposes. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

6. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@phonixplayer.com.
